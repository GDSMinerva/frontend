FAQs

How do I verify ATS compliance? expand_more

CareerSeed checks your resume for formatting and structure issues that many ATS systems reject. You get a compatibility rating based on common ATS requirements.

What is a good ATS score? expand_more

A score of 80% or higher is generally considered strong — it indicates your resume parses well and has good keyword and semantic fit for the role.

Can you parse PDF resumes? expand_more

Yes. We support PDF and DOCX. We also check that your file is readable by common systems (e.g. not image-only PDFs).

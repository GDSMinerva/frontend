System FAQs

How do I verify ATS compliance?

expand_more
Each Applicant Tracking System employs distinct filtering algorithms. BridgeToJob's scanner detects formatting anomalies that render your CV illegible to these systems. We provide a compatibility rating based on aggregate data from top ATS providers.

What defines an optimal ATS score?

expand_more
A rating of 80% or higher is considered optimal. It signifies your resume is correctly parsed and contains the necessary keyword density and semantic relevance for the target job description.

Is ATS scanning universal?

expand_more
Affirmative. Approximately 99% of Fortune 500 companies and most mid-sized enterprises utilize automated tracking systems to filter candidates before human review occurs.

Can the system parse PDF files?

expand_more
Modern systems can generally parse PDFs unless they are flattened images. However, BridgeToJob's verification engine checks your file structure to ensure maximum readability across legacy and modern platforms.
